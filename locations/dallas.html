Serving DFW libraries

Oklahoma-based, Texas-ready

Oklahoma City is just 3.5 hours from Dallas — and we make regular runs to serve DFW-area libraries during summer reading season. A modest travel fee applies, but many Dallas-area libraries find it well worth it for a performer who specializes exclusively in library programming.

DFW is a large metro and we can coordinate multi-branch visits efficiently. Irving Public Library, Garland Public Library, Plano Public Library, Denton Public Library, and Richardson Public Library have all been served. We're also happy to work with smaller municipal libraries in the DFW suburbs.

The best approach for DFW: submit one inquiry with all your branch names and preferred date range. We'll route an efficient 2–3 day DFW visit and send back a proposed schedule.
