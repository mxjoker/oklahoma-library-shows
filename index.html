Dinosaur Adventure: Magic Show

Children45–60 min
Step back in time for a prehistoric adventure filled with laughter, magic, and audience participation! Designed for the 2026 Summer Reading Theme, blending hilarious moments with jaw-dropping illusions as children help uncover the secrets of the dinosaurs.

Learning outcomes

Encourages curiosity, imagination, and participation while inspiring kids to think creatively about science, history, and storytelling.

Professor Buckets Bubble Show

Children45–60 minSTEAM
Join the eccentric Professor Buckets for a high-energy science adventure exploring the world of bubbles! Combines humor, hands-on demonstrations, and amazing stunts — including the unforgettable Kid-in-a-Bubble finale.

Learning outcomes

Introduces the scientific method in a fun, accessible way. Promotes observation, experimentation, and curiosity about physics and chemistry.

$385 / program

Hands-on Magic Trick Workshop

Children45–60 min
Kids become magicians in this exciting, interactive workshop! Participants learn easy, age-appropriate magic tricks using everyday objects while exploring the secrets of presentation, confidence, and creativity. They leave ready to perform for friends and family!

Learning outcomes

Encourages critical thinking, communication skills, and creativity while reinforcing focus and sequencing.

$385 / program
🎈

Hands-on Balloon Twisting Workshop

All ages45–60 min
Participants learn the basics of balloon twisting as they create simple animals, hats, and other shapes. This workshop fosters creativity, coordination, and patience while giving every child a tangible, self-made creation to take home.

Learning outcomes

Develops fine motor skills, hand-eye coordination, focus, and creative problem-solving. Builds confidence through accomplishment.

$385 / program
✏️

Story Doodles Draw-Along

Children45–60 min
An eccentric artiste brings stories to life through live drawing! Children draw along as the tale unfolds, creating their own illustrations to match the story. Encourages creativity, storytelling, and artistic expression — a one-of-a-kind program.

Learning outcomes

Promotes literacy, listening comprehension, sequencing, and creative expression through interactive storytelling.

$385 / program

The process

Simple booking, zero stress

We know librarians are busy. Here's exactly how it works — from first inquiry to show day.

1

Submit your request

Tell us which programs you want and your preferred dates. No commitment required — just a quick inquiry.

2

3
