2. Start: Kids gather outside — anticipation builds
3. Foam begins: Machines start — slow at first, then overwhelming in the best way
4. Peak foam: Dance party energy — kids run, jump, make foam "snowballs"
5. Wind-down: Machines slow, kids towel off, parents grab photos

## Special Logistics Section (this show needs its own section)
**FOAM PARTY REQUIREMENTS:**
- Outdoor space: minimum 30×30 ft flat area
- Water source: outdoor hose connection within 50 feet
- Surface: grass or concrete — no sloped surfaces
- Weather: Cancel policy — Joe contacts library 24 hours before if forecast is unfavorable; rescheduling is coordinated directly
- Participant note: Kids should wear clothes they're OK getting wet/foamy
- Parent note: Towels strongly recommended; sunscreen before the event

**Foam Calendar Note:**
"Unlike Joe's other shows, the Foam Party runs on a separate outdoor calendar because dates
are weather-dependent and require advance weather monitoring. When you inquire about a
Foam Party, include your top 2–3 preferred dates and we'll match you to available foam calendar slots."
