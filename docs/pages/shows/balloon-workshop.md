## What Happens (narrative)
Walk through:
1. Intro: Joe shows finished examples — animal, hat, flower — and explains "you're going to make one of these"
2. Balloon basics: how to inflate, how to hold, the first twist
3. Shape #1: Simple dog or sword (easy win — builds confidence)
4. Shape #2: More complex shape — hat or flower
5. Customization: Kids personalize — colors, names, accessories
6. Parade: Optional — everyone holds up their creation at the end

Key point: tangible takeaway is the star of this show. It's Instagram gold and word-of-mouth gold.
