## What Happens (narrative)
Walk through:
1. Setup: Kids get paper and crayons/markers as they enter
2. Intro: Joe introduces the story and the first drawing prompt
3. Story begins: Joe draws on a large pad/easel while narrating — kids draw along
4. Turns and twists: The story takes unexpected directions — new prompts, new drawings
5. Character moments: Kids' drawings become part of the story (Joe holds them up, reacts)
6. Resolution: The story concludes — every kid holds up their completed illustration

Key differentiator: This is the ONLY show in the lineup where the audience creates something that becomes part of the performance.
