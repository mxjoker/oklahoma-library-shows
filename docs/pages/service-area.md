### 2. Oklahoma Coverage (Primary)

**Zone 1 — No Travel Fee**
Cities: Oklahoma City metro (Edmond, Yukon, Moore, Midwest City, Mustang, Del City, Choctaw), Norman, Tulsa metro (Broken Arrow, Owasso, Jenks, Sand Springs, Bixby)
> "If your library is in the OKC or Tulsa metro area — there's no travel fee. Period."

**Zone 2 — Standard Travel Fee**
Rural Oklahoma outside the metro areas. Fee calculated on distance from OKC.
> "For rural Oklahoma libraries, we calculate a travel fee based on distance. In most cases, this is a one-time charge regardless of how many shows are booked on that trip."

**Zone 3 — Cluster Discount (Rural)**
> "Two or more rural libraries booking shows on the same trip? We split the travel fee across all libraries. Ask about coordinating with neighboring library systems."

### 4. How Travel Fees Are Calculated
Transparent explanation:
- Base rate: distance from OKC
- Reduction: multiple shows on same trip divide the travel cost
