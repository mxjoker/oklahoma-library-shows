The 2027 CSLP Theme

The 2027 Collaborative Summer Library Program theme puts your entire summer in detective mode. Mystery, suspense, and the thrill of the unknown — every program becomes an investigation, every book a case file, every patron a junior detective.

Joe Coover is already adapting all six Funky Monkey Events programs for the 2027 mystery theme. Magic shows become whodunit adventures. Story Doodles unfolds as a live mystery. Even the Balloon Workshop gets detective hats and magnifying glasses.

2027 Programs

Six shows, all going mystery in 2027

Magic Show

2027: A whodunit adventure — every trick is a clue

Audiences help the detective-magician crack the case. Each illusion reveals a new clue until the mystery is finally — magically — solved. Perfect for all ages.
🫧

Bubble Show

2027: Professor Buckets investigates the mysterious science of bubbles

The eccentric professor takes a detective's approach to bubble physics — why do they float? Why do they pop? The Kid-in-a-Bubble finale stays. It's too good to change.
🪄

Magic Workshop

2027: Train young detectives — learn the tricks of misdirection

Kids learn real magic tricks themed around detective skills — misdirection, sleight of hand, the art of the reveal. They leave as junior detective-magicians ready to perform.
🎈

Balloon Workshop

2027: Twist detective hats, magnifying glasses & mystery props

Every child leaves with a balloon creation they made themselves — in 2027, detective hats, magnifying glasses, and evidence envelopes. Zero balloons go to waste.
✏️

Story Doodles

2027: Draw the crime scene as the mystery unfolds live on stage

A mystery story is drawn live — kids draw along as suspects appear, clues are revealed, and the detective closes in. Can the audience crack the case before the artist finishes?
🌊

Foam Party

2027: Theme-agnostic — the mystery is how fun it's going to be

The Foam Party is the Foam Party — no theme adaptation needed. It's the perfect outdoor summer finale that brings your whole community together regardless of the CSLP theme.
