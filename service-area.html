Travel fee policy

No travel fee for libraries in the OKC metro area. For all other areas, travel is billed at the current IRS standard mileage rate — always disclosed upfront, no surprises on your invoice.

For multi-branch systems and libraries that want to combine multiple shows on one trip, travel fees are consolidated across the entire schedule rather than charged per show. We're happy to work with you to route efficiently and keep costs down — just mention that in your inquiry.
