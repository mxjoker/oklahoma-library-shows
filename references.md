  - New theme announced each year — update site annually
  - Libraries plan summer programming around CSLP themes

## ODL Program Descriptions (Source of Truth for Show Copy)
Use these exact descriptions on show pages — they are Joe's approved ODL submissions:

**Dinosaur Adventure: Magic Show**
"Step back in time for a prehistoric adventure filled with laughter, magic, and audience
participation! This brand-new show is designed especially for the 2026 Summer Reading Theme,
blending hilarious moments with jaw-dropping illusions. Children and families alike will roar
with delight as they help the magician uncover the secrets of the dinosaurs in this fast-paced,
interactive performance that makes learning about the past magical."
Outcomes: Encourages curiosity, imagination, and participation while inspiring kids to think
creatively about science, history, and storytelling.
Learning: Promotes reading and learning through themed storytelling, vocabulary enrichment,
and curiosity about science and history.

**Professor Buckets Bubble Show**
"Join the eccentric Professor Buckets for a high-energy science adventure exploring the fun and
fascinating world of bubbles! This zany show combines humor, hands-on demonstrations, and amazing
bubble stunts — including the Kid-in-a-Bubble finale."
Outcomes: Introduces the scientific method in a fun, accessible way. Promotes observation,
experimentation, and curiosity about physics and chemistry.
Learning: Reinforces STEM learning through interactive science demonstrations.


**Hands-on Magic Trick Workshop**
"Kids become magicians in this exciting, interactive workshop! Participants learn easy,
age-appropriate magic tricks using everyday objects while exploring the secrets of presentation,
confidence, and creativity. By the end of the session, they'll have tricks they can perform
for friends and family — and the confidence to share them!"
Outcomes: Builds fine motor skills, memory, public speaking, and creative problem-solving.
Fosters imagination and self-confidence.
Learning: Encourages critical thinking, communication skills, and creativity while reinforcing
focus and sequencing.

**Hands-on Balloon Twisting Workshop**
"Hands-on fun for kids of all ages! Participants learn the basics of balloon twisting as they
create simple animals, hats, and other shapes. This workshop fosters creativity, coordination,
and patience while giving every child a tangible, self-made balloon creation to take home.
A perfect blend of artistry and play."
Outcomes: Develops fine motor skills, hand-eye coordination, focus, and creative
problem-solving. Builds confidence through accomplishment.
Learning: Supports cognitive and motor skill development through hands-on learning.

**Story Doodles Draw-Along**
"An eccentric artiste brings stories to life through live drawing! Children are invited to draw
along as the tale unfolds, creating their own illustrations to match the story. This
one-of-a-kind performance encourages creativity, storytelling, and artistic expression. Kids
can participate by drawing, or simply enjoy the magical unfolding of the story as art and
imagination combine onstage."
Outcomes: Promotes literacy, listening comprehension, sequencing, and creative expression
through interactive storytelling.
Learning: Strengthens reading comprehension and narrative understanding while encouraging
visual creativity and fine motor skills.
